Building a Fashion Advisory Chatbot

Author:
Minh Dua
Current Company/School:
FPT Software Can Tho
Short introduction:
Full-stack developer

The rapidly evolving world of fashion, staying updated with trends and making the right style choices can be overwhelming. To address this challenge, I embarked on an ambitious project to build a fashion advisory chatbot, harnessing the power of artificial intelligence.

The Core of the Project:

The chatbot system is developed using the Rasa framework, known for its effectiveness in creating conversational AI. It's coupled with advanced Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), crucial for processing and understanding fashion-related imagery.

Objective and Innovation:

The primary goal was to create a chatbot that not only converses but also provides fashion recommendations based on user preferences and queries...

Reflection and Future Prospects:

This project was not just a technological achievement but a step towards transforming how we interact with fashion digitally. Looking ahead, the potential expansions include personalized shopping experiences and integrating real-time fashion trends.
