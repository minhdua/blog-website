University Admissions Assistance Chatbot

Author:
Minh Dua
Current Company/School:
FPT Software Can Tho
Short introduction:
Full-stack developer

In today's digital age, the search for accurate and timely university admissions information can be daunting for prospective students. Recognizing this challenge, I developed a University Admissions Assistance Chatbot to streamline the information-gathering process, making it more efficient and user-friendly.

Project Overview:

The chatbot system is built on the Rasa framework, renowned for its conversational AI capabilities. It leverages PostgreSQL for data management and employs Python programming language for backend processes. The chatbot uniquely integrates with university enrollment websites to provide real-time, updated admission information to users.

Goals and Innovation:

The main objective was to create a chatbot that could assist high school students with various admissions queries, offering detailed information about university programs, application procedures, and enrollment criteria...

Impact and Future Directions:

The implementation of this chatbot has significantly improved the efficiency of university admissions processes, providing students with immediate access to essential information. This project not only demonstrates the potential of AI in education but also lays the groundwork for further advancements in digital student support systems.
